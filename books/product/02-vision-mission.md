# 02 Vision and Mission

Status: Draft v1.0

## Vision

Enable every organisation to manage its visual communication heritage from one platform.

## Mission

Turn isolated orders into structured projects with reusable assets, clear validation, production tracking and simple reorder.

## Product promise

All visual communication, history and reorder capability in one place.

## Strategic direction

DBG Platform is built first for Design by Greg, while keeping an architecture that can later support other actors.
