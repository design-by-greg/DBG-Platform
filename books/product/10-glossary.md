# 10 Glossary

Status: Draft v1.0

## Organisation

Main business entity using DBG Platform.

## Brand

Visual identity owned by an Organisation.

## Workspace

Internal space used to group Projects.

## Project

User-facing work unit.

## Asset

Reusable object owned by an Organisation.

## Resource

File attached to an Asset.

## Product Library

Private catalogue of validated products owned by an Organisation.

## Order

Commercial event linked to a Project.

## Production Job

Operational task required to produce an Order.
