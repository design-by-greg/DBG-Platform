# 04 Principles

Status: Draft v1.0

1. Organisation owns its data.
2. Project is the user entry point.
3. Order is an event.
4. Validated products become reusable assets.
5. Reorder starts from Product Library.
6. Suppliers are replaceable.
7. Business domain does not depend on technology.
8. Important decisions are documented.
9. MVP stays simple.
10. Every feature reduces friction.
