# 05 Personas

Status: Draft v1.0

## Company manager

Needs simple ordering, reorder history, team clothing and reliable follow-up.

## Club manager

Needs seasonal products, public or private views, member orders and simple reorder.

## Association manager

Needs event projects, flyers, textile, volunteers and sponsor materials.

## Partner

Needs catalogue access, price preparation, order tracking and customer follow-up.

## Internal DBG team

Needs CRM, BAT workflow, production tracking, asset management and customer history.
